How to Play


Welcome to Word Adventurer!


Press the letter keys on your keyboard to guess the hidden word. Be careful! Your friend Shawrvind--who is floating in the sky suspended by 10 balloons--is depending on you! For every incorrect letter guessed, you will pop one of Shawrvind's balloons. There is a scary beast waiting for Shawrvind to fall into his jaws if he looses all 10 balloons.The number of ballons left, and your highscore of how many balloons you were able to save by the end will show up on the top right corner. Be it a win or a defeat, you will be able to view the dictionary definition of the hiddden word by clicking the hyperlink which will appear on the screeen at the end of each game. If you are enjoying the game, click the next word button to keep going!


Will you be able to save Shawrvind? Return to Home and click Play Now to get started!


About Us


Hi everyone, thank you for joining us to play Word Adventurer! This game was developed by Emily, Julia, and Teresa, three incoming university students in Waterloo, Canada. The purpose of this game is to create a fun and engaging environment for English learners, or anyone seeking to expand their vocbulary. We hope you enjoy!
