How to Play

Welcome to Word Adventurer!

Press the letter keys on your keyboard or click the screen to guess the hidden word. Be careful! Your friend Sharvind ― who is floating in the sky suspended by 10 balloons ― is depending on you! For every incorrect letter guessed, 1 of Sharvind's balloons will pop. There is a scary beast waiting for Sharvind to fall into his jaws if he loses all 10 balloons.The number of ballons left, and your highscore of how many balloons you were able to save by the end will show up on the top right corner. Be it a win or a defeat, you will be able to view the dictionary definition of the hiddden word by clicking the hyperlink which will appear on the screeen at the end of each game. If you are enjoying the game, click the next word button to keep going!
